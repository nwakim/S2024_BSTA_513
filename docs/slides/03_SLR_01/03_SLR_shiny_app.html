Life Expectancy Simple Linear Regression

Let’s try to find a model fit with the lowest score!

Click two distinct points of the plot to draw a line. Try to minimize the score (at the top of the plot) using different lines. To create a new line just click on the plot more than twice. In this activity, the number we get is our “score.” In statistics, this score is actually called the sum of squared errors (SSE). Were you able to get the best-fit line presented in class?
